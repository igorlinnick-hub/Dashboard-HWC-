Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026  ·  HelloMetrics by KINNIL

1. Overview

HelloMetrics ("we", "our", or "us") is a dashboard application that connects to Facebook and Meta platforms to aggregate and display analytics metrics. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook account or Facebook Pages to HelloMetrics, we may access and store the following data through the Meta API:

  • Facebook Page names and IDs
  • Page engagement metrics (reach, impressions, likes, comments, shares)
  • Post performance data and insights
  • Ad account metrics (spend, reach, performance) if authorized
  • Basic profile information required for authentication

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your Facebook metrics inside your HelloMetrics dashboard
  • To generate reports and analytics summaries for your accounts
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Facebook Permissions We Request

HelloMetrics requests only the permissions necessary to read your analytics data. These may include:

  • pages_show_list — to list your Facebook Pages
  • pages_read_engagement — to read engagement metrics
  • read_insights — to access page and post insights
  • ads_read — to read advertising performance data
  • business_management — to access business account data

We do not post, publish, or modify any content on your behalf.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized users only. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. Metric data may be cached temporarily to improve dashboard performance.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke HelloMetrics' access to your Facebook data at any time through your Facebook App Settings. Upon revocation, we will cease pulling new data from your account.

8. Third-Party Services

HelloMetrics integrates with the Meta Graph API. Your use of Facebook and Meta services is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

HelloMetrics is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
